First AID Training

Emergency Response

First Aid is a core and crucial activity of the Indian Red Cross Society (IRCS), forming the foundation of the Red Cross and Red Crescent Movement. It empowers communities and strengthens humanitarian response across India.

The National Headquarters regularly conducts workshops and trainings in basic First Aid to update volunteers with the latest techniques and trends. These sessions help build capacity at both national and branch levels.

Trained personnel are deployed in local, national, and international emergencies, responding swiftly to humanitarian needs and offering crucial day-to-day support for the most vulnerable communities.
